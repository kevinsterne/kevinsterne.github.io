I’m a business writer at Challenger, Gray & Christmas in Chicago and contributer at Chicago Health Magazine and Substream Magazine. I've also written for The Rumpus, Psych Central and Reviewing the Evidence, among others.

I reside in Chicago and earned my M.A. from DePaul University. You might find me writing at Dark Matter Coffee or running by the lake (Michigan).

For readers I suggest Rachel Kushner’s The Flamethrowers; and for listeners I recommend Sufjan Stevens and Sigur Ros.
