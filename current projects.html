LeFawn Magazine issue 6

LeFawn Magazine, is a zine of fiction and art, including corporate satire, list pieces and immoral musings. Every issue is chocked full of fun psuedoscience and the ever-evolving narrative of the magazine's editor, Togna Bologna, and his attempts to rid himself of his angry past life, LeFawn. Issue 6 contains McSweeny's rejects, never-before-seen pieces, plenty of politcal satire and completely reworked pieces from the Shuga Records blog.
